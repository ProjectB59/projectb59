Privacy & Cookie Policy

Last updated: 1 July 2026

Project B59 (“the Vault”), operated by Modulo 59, is built to be privacy-preserving by default. This page explains exactly what the site stores and what it loads. We keep it short because there isn’t much to say.

What we store on your device

The Vault uses your browser’s local storage only for essential, functional purposes:

  • Preferences — your radio station and volume, and your choice to dismiss this notice.

This information stays in your browser, is never transmitted to us, and you can clear it at any time from your browser settings.

What we do not do

  • No advertising or tracking cookies.
  • No analytics or behavioural profiling.
  • No selling, renting, or sharing of personal data — ever.
  • No accounts, and we do not ask for personal information to browse the archive.

Third-party content

Some features load content from third parties, which may set their own cookies or log your IP address under their own policies:

  • Google Fonts — typography is served by Google’s font CDN.
  • Channel 59 stream — the live video embed loads from our streaming server and its network provider only when you open it.
  • Live IRC — the optional live chat connects to a third-party IRC web client (KiwiIRC / Libera.Chat) only when you use it.
  • Linked sites — external links (GitHub, the arcade, social) are governed by their own policies.

Donations

Any voluntary donation you send goes to a public Solana address (Dra35HtSDPBPh4cV58jmTuQSWsyHpR7ZVh8HfxM9tSq7). Blockchain transactions are public by nature; we do not collect any personal details as part of a donation.

Your choices

You can clear the Vault’s local storage at any time via your browser, decline third-party embeds by simply not opening them, and block cookies globally in your browser settings. The core archive works fully without any of the optional third-party features.

Changes

If this policy changes, we will update the date above. Continued use of the site after a change means you accept the updated policy.
